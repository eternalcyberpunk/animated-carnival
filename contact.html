Your first visit

What to expect.

If you've never been to a medspa before, here's the short version of what happens on your first consultation.

01

A real conversation

We sit down for 30 minutes. Jessie asks what's bothering you, what you've tried, what you're hoping to change. She listens more than she talks.

02

No pressure plan

She'll write up a treatment plan — what she'd recommend, in what order, for what investment. You go home with it. No upselling, no "book today for a discount."

03

You decide

If you want to book, great — we'll set up a separate appointment for your first treatment. If you want to wait, or try a different place, that's completely fine too.
