Why this exists

The gap between knowing and doing.

Most aesthetic courses teach you where to put the needle. Very few teach you when not to inject at all, how to have the consultation conversation, or how to talk a client out of something they shouldn't do.

That's the gap Jessie spent a decade closing — first for herself, then for the hundreds of injectors she's mentored. Boon Trainings fill in what the weekend certification courses leave out.

Trainings are small (max four people), hands-on with live models, and built around real casework. You leave with confidence, not just a certificate.

Questions

What trainees ask.

Do I need experience to apply?
For Foundations, no — just a valid RN, NP, PA, MD, or DO license. For Advanced Intensive, we require one full year of hands-on injection experience. For Mentorship, two-plus years.
How big are the groups?
Four people max, always. That's a hard ceiling — any larger and the hands-on practice falls apart.
Do I get a certificate?
Yes. But more importantly, we're happy to be a professional reference. Many of our trainees stay in touch for years after the training — that ongoing support is the real value.
Is housing or travel included?
Not included, but we'll send recommendations for local hotels in Claremont Village. Most people stay 2–3 nights and make a trip out of it.
How do I apply?
Fill out the application form below. Jessie reviews every application personally and responds within 5 business days. Once accepted, you'll receive a deposit invoice to reserve your spot.
