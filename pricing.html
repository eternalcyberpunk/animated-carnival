Fine print

Our policies.

Cancellations

Please give us 24 hours notice. Same-day cancellations incur a $50 fee that goes toward your next visit. No-shows are billed the full consultation value.

Price changes

Prices listed are current as of 2026 and may change. Any active treatment plan will honor the price quoted at your consultation for 60 days.

Touch-ups

First-time neuromodulator touch-ups within 2 weeks are free. Filler touch-ups at the 4-week mark are billed at $150, not a full syringe price.

Refunds

Neuromodulator and filler treatments are not refundable, but we stand behind our work. If you're not happy at your follow-up, we'll make it right.

Gift cards

Available in any amount, never expire, can be applied toward any service. Contact the studio to purchase.

Loyalty

We don't do formal loyalty programs. What we do: return clients often get the most favorable pricing, and we remember you.
