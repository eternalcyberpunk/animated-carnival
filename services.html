Before you book

A few honest answers.

Do I need a consultation before my first treatment?
Yes. Always. Jessie doesn't inject on the first visit — we meet, we talk, we look at your face in natural light, we make a plan together. You go home and decide. It's the way all aesthetic medicine should work.
How do I choose the right service?
You don't have to. Book a consultation and describe what you'd like to feel different about — Jessie will tell you what (if anything) makes sense to try. Sometimes the answer is "a really good skincare routine first."
Can I combine treatments in one visit?
Sometimes, but usually no. Jessie prefers to space things out so you can see what's working. Layering too many things at once makes it harder to know what made the difference.
Do you offer packages or memberships?
Not currently. Packages push you toward treatments you may not need. Instead we offer Cherry and Affirm for financing when you want it.
What if I don't like the results?
Most of our treatments are either reversible (hyaluronic acid fillers can be dissolved) or temporary (Botox wears off in 3–4 months). We'd rather under-treat than overdo it, so we start conservative. If you wish we'd done more, we add more at the follow-up.
